What's PlantLife??

PlantLife was created out of a passion to help better organize your home life. More and more people are stuck at home these days and are turning to house plants as a way to stay connected with the outdoors and liven up their in home lifestyle. If you are someone who has more than one plant and have trouble remembering which plant to water when, or even if you only have one plant but like me can never remember the last time you actually watered it so you end up over watering. Then this site is for you. Here you can track, journal and record anything you want. Save reminders to your calendar and check which plants need watering today. Take advantage of our journaling feature to add photos and observations about the health and temperment of your specific plant. This should help you figure out if your plant likes more or less sunlight, more or less water and even catch problems before they spread and your plant dies. We hope that this site in some small way can help make your life a little less stressful and a little more filled with life, love and happiness as you enjoy wherever you call home.
